St Valentine’s day 2012

We celebrated this special day in a special way. Everyone was given in the morning a paper heart with a number on it and this s/he put on. This was used for special St Valentine’s post. We could send a card to “our favourite numbers” and sign the card with our number, so it was a secret. :) In IPC lessons Y1 and Y2 baked some sweet hearts for themselves and also for parents and teachers.




Developing literacy skills

The World Education Games 2012

On 6th and 7th March we took part in Word Spelling and Maths Days. It was for the first time the school participated also in the Spelling Day. Dressed as avatars from the web application children competed over two days with each other and with other children from around the world. After first winnings and losses of World Spelling Day, the players were a bit tired. They got well back on track when Mr. Krudenc came to support their efforts and was competing against other parents and teachers. Thanks to all parents who supported children. We thank especially Mr. Valenta, Mr. Keder, and Mr. Ocvirk for their courage and efforts. Congratulations go to Mr. Krudenc - the winner of the Maths challenge in the teachers and parents category.


MP2 – Reggae Musicians

On Thursday 8th March the MP2 children were visited by two members of a musical band from Olomouc called Homebwoy Rasta. They came to talk to the children about one of the musical genres they had been learning about recently in their IPC lessons – about reggae. The children were also pretty interested in the quite common hairstyle of reggae musicians and asked many interesting questions about the dreadlocks. They, of course, had a chance to play with them which was a great experience for everyone! ☺
