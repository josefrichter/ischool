MP2 – The Active Planet (Science)

The children from MP2 learnt how to conduct an experiment and carried out several experiments themselves through which they discovered what happens inside the volcano when it erupts, what happens when a crack appears in the Earth´s crust, what gases are given off during the volcanic eruption, that not all the liquids flow at the same speed and not all the solids melt at the same temperature. They also learnt about temperature, states of matter, changes in matter and changes in materials. I am sure that now they understand things around us a little bit more again! ☺

“THINGS PEOPLE DO”: Entry Point

“There are so many jobs to choose from when you grow up! – I start our new IPC Unit.– What do you want to do in future?” While Adamek P immediately replies “Fireman!”, Julinka becomes thoughtful “I’m still not sure who I want to be. There are so many jobs!”

During this IPC unit we will be learning more about how grown-ups spend their days and how the people were working in ancient times. There will be also time for Music and Art. On Thursday 10th November afternoon our classroom turned into an imaginary city of Schoolomouc. There were no more children, but many grown-ups. They were busy with their work from morning till evening. Honzik was a doctor, prescribing medicine, Zuzka helped Julca at the Veterinary Clinic, Adamek V was giving out pizza at lunch breaks, Matysek was in charge of clean rooms.

Eda was spending his “days” exploring black holes and stars through the telescope. Katya was training her volleyball team after work. But all the city began to panic as soon as the fire started at Adamek’s Pizza, Adamek P, a fireman, was saving house from disaster. …And that was just the start. More fun is expected during next 4 weeks.
