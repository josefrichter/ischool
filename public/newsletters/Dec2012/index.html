Body

For Milepost 2 children, the first term of this school year was in sign of getting to know each other not only with the new teacher, but also with three classmates, who surely enriched the friendly relations earlier established as well as the international atmosphere in the classroom. Together we passed the first unit "Shaping up" in IPC. We examined how our body works, what we should do to maintain their health and what we should rather avoid. An unforgettable experience was doctor Maskova´s invitation, when pupils have a unique opportunity to find valuable information about the human body in her consulting room. Everyone tried ECG, pressure measurement, looking through radiographs and with pride everyone received the ultrasound picture of his/her heart. See how we enjoyed visiting cardiology in Litovel!

Halloween in Ostrava: Yes, we can!

On November 30 at 6.30 a.m. Olomouc Main Train Station was full of ghosts and witches and all other "evil spirits": our school was getting ready to the first ever day trip to International School of Ostrava. Children stayed with their age group as we joined Ostrava life - lessons, breaks and even snacks. Both schools came together again for the Halloween disco. Our children impressed others with the aerobic dance they had created on their own during lunch breaks, and Romanek's performance got even more applause. There were different opinions from the children about the trip, but we hope they also realized that's important to speak a language everybody understands and there lots of small things they can like their school for.











Czech Programme - Theatre Performance

At the beginning of November the children from the whole school (Preschool and Junior School) visited Divadlo hudby Olomouc and enjoyed a great performance called „How to Save Our Earth“. Throughout the performance the children were introduced to a variety of ways in which they themselves can help save the Earth and become more environmentally friendly. And in addition to this, they also had a good laugh.

MP3 - The School Olympics

In September MP3 children organized an opening ceremony of our own School Olympics. Thanks to our enthusiastic children who did a great job and prepared fantastic performances within a short period of time, delicious healthy refreshment provided by our equally enthusiastic parents and beautiful weather we all have something to remember. The MP3 Olympic mission was accomplished by The School Olympic Games during which the children were tought an Olympic song composed by MP3 children, competed in lots of funny indoor games and received handmade Olympic medals. All the children learnt that it is not important to win but take part.
